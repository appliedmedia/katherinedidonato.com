About Katherine

A collaborative approach, grounded in CBT.

Katherine DiDonato, PhD is a licensed psychologist with over 25 years of experience. She uses a collaborative, goal-oriented approach to help clients recognize unhelpful patterns of thinking, manage painful emotions, and build lasting skills for coping with difficult circumstances.

All sessions are held virtually, making it easy to access care from wherever you are in New Jersey or New York.

Areas of focus

What Katherine treats.

Anxiety & Panic Disorders
CBT and exposure-based techniques to break the cycle of avoidance and reclaim your world.
Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder
Specialized Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP), the gold-standard treatment for OCD.
Depression
Active, structured work to shift the patterns in thinking and behavior that maintain low mood.
Pregnancy, Prenatal & Postpartum
Expert support through the full perinatal period, including anxiety, depression, birth trauma, and loss.
Infertility
A space to process grief, fear, and uncertainty during one of life’s most emotionally demanding experiences.
