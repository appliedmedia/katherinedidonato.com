# FAQs Page

## Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes. Katherine offers a free 15-minute phone consultation for anyone who wants a brief, no-commitment conversation before booking a first session. This is a good opportunity to ask questions and get a sense of whether Katherine is the right fit.

[Book a free consultation](<https://secure.helloalma.com/providers/katherine-didonato/>)

## What does a first session look like?

The intake appointment is 45 minutes. It is a full first session: Katherine will ask about what brings you in, your history, and your goals for therapy. If you are using insurance, have your information handy before the session.

[Book an intake appointment](<https://secure.helloalma.com/providers/katherine-didonato/>)

## How much do sessions cost?

Out-of-network sessions are $140 to $200 per session. Katherine also offers a sliding scale for clients with financial need.

If you are using insurance, your out-of-pocket cost will depend on your specific plan. Katherine can provide a superbill (a detailed receipt) that you can submit to your insurance for possible out-of-network reimbursement.

## Do you offer a sliding scale?

Yes, sliding scale fees are available based on financial need. Reach out to discuss.

## Do you offer in-person sessions?

No. All sessions are held virtually via telehealth. This allows Katherine to work with clients throughout New Jersey and New York from wherever is most convenient for you.

## Where are you licensed?

Katherine is licensed in New Jersey and New York. She also holds a PSYPACT license, which authorizes telehealth practice in an additional 44 participating states.

## Who do you work with?

Katherine works with individual adults, including young adults (18 to 24), adults (25 to 64), and seniors (65 and older).

Please reach out directly to discuss cancellation and rescheduling policies before booking.
